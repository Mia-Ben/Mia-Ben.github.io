New York Fashion Week

Oscar de la Renta Fall/Winter 2025/2026 show

Oscar de la Renta Fall/Winter 2025/2026 womenswear ready-to-wear collection by Laura Kim and Fernando Garcia was presented at NYFW. These two did not disappoint.

The presenting started off with a black and white palette occasionally spiced up with the sparkling sequin.
Later, black and white were joined by brighter colors, such as yellow, sky-blue and red.
The next part stood out with mixed prints and patterns that screamed elegance and grace.
The collection was finished with golden and silver theme.

The designers approached the seasonal mood seriously. Even the bright colors were as cold as snow in Central Park.
The traditional flowers weren't left out. Flowers were printed on white fabric symbolizing springcoming as flowers on the snow.
